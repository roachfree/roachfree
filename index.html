USER GUIDE

Follow these steps to stay Roachfree

  • Dry the area the paste has to be applied on.
  • Take out some paste on the tip of your finger.
  • Apply the paste on the wall skirting 6'-10' above the skirting.
  • Let the paste dry and do not let it come in contact with water.
  • Enjoy Rochfree spaces.
  • Remember to reapply after 3 months.

It is necessary to remove the old paste and apply fresh one after 3 months of use even if you do not see cockroaches. This prevents them from devoloping immunity towards the paste.
