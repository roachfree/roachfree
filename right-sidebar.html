Our Story

Are cockroaches infesting your home and you are facing difficulties to get rid of them?
Let's put an end to all those woes with ROACHFREE - an environment friendly cockroach repellent.

Special features

Rochfree is a well tested product which has many satisfies consumers. It is :

  • Non-toxic and can be used in places like a cockroch infested fridge or adjacent to open food.
  • Odourless and ideal for air conditioned space, where the foul odour of chemical sprays linger long after application
  • Hassle free in application
  • Eco- friendly and safe
  • Suitable to use everywhere including kitchen, canteens, bakeries etc.
  • Never tested on animals

Roachfree is a product of a young mind - 17 years old Aryaman Taparia, who comprehended a two-fold problem related to cockroaches:
Firstly, he harboured an immense fear for roaches, finding it difficult to fall asleep if he knew there was one in his house.
Secondly, the toxic gas released from chemical repellents gave him an allergic reaction.

As a result, in year 2016, he devoted himself to developing a product that could deal with both the issues simultaneously. After a lot of trials and errors he finally reached to a solution that was chemical free and odourless. Being a meticulous person , He wanted the packaging to be such that it can be used easily by the consumer. Thus,he cut the solution into small bricks which can cover a significant area and can sustain for a longer time.
This lead to the developing of of Roachfree-an odourless, all natural, herbal solution, in the year 2020.
